How we work

The right test for what you need to learn.

Not every question gets answered the same way. We choose the test type that gets you the signal you need — and only that.

Concept testing

"Does this idea resonate at all?"

Fast, scrappy, live sessions with real customers. We find out quickly if you're onto something — or need to pivot — before investing further.

Demand testing

"Will people actually take action to get this?"

We launch a landing page, ad, offer, or prototype that customers can actually respond to — then measure what they do, not what they say. Clicks, sign-ups, bookings. Revealed intent, not stated intent.

Engagement testing

"Will they follow through when it requires real effort?"

Demand tells you if people intend to use something. Engagement tells you if they actually will — especially when it takes time, repetition, or commitment.

Benefit testing

"If we deliver this, will it actually help them?"

Before building the real thing, we deliver the experience by hand — manually, behind the scenes — to confirm the promised outcome actually holds up for real customers. No assumptions. Observed results.
